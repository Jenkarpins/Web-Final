Guides & Tips

  • Get Runes Fast: Head to Mohgwyn Palace early via Varre’s questline to farm Albinaurics with AoE.
  • Missables: Always back up your save before fighting NPCs or triggering ending events.
  • Boss Order: Tackle legacy dungeons like Stormveil and Leyndell before wandering too deep into endgame areas.
  • Secret Endings: Ranni and Frenzied Flame endings require early quest decisions — plan ahead.
